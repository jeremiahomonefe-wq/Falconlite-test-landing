General

Falconlite is a global financial platform built for Africans. We provide USD virtual cards, multi-currency wallets, global bank account details, crypto payment support, and fast currency conversion — all in one app. Whether you're a freelancer getting paid internationally, a student paying tuition abroad, or a business accepting global payments, Falconlite makes it seamless.
Creating an account takes less than 5 minutes. Click "Get Started" on our homepage, enter your email address and create a password, then verify your identity with a government-issued ID. Once approved — usually within minutes — you'll have full access to all Falconlite features.
Falconlite supports over 30 currencies including USD, GBP, EUR, NGN, GHS, KES, ZAR, and more. You can hold balances in multiple currencies simultaneously in your multi-currency wallet and convert between them at competitive real-time exchange rates.
Falconlite is currently available in over 150 countries, with primary support for Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, and other African nations. We're continuously expanding our coverage. Check our website or app for the most up-to-date list of supported countries.
Falconlite is a financial technology company, not a bank. Banking services are provided by our licensed banking partners. Your funds are held in segregated accounts with our partner banks and are protected in accordance with applicable regulations.

Payments

Most international transfers are processed within minutes to a few hours. SWIFT transfers may take 1–3 business days depending on the destination bank. Transfers between Falconlite accounts are instant, 24/7.
Falconlite charges a flat fee starting from $1 for most international transfers, with no hidden charges. The exact fee depends on the transfer amount, destination country, and your plan. You'll always see the full fee breakdown before confirming any transaction.
Yes. With Falconlite's Global Accounts feature, you get dedicated USD, GBP, and EUR account details (routing number, sort code, IBAN) that you can share with international clients. They can pay you via bank transfer just like paying a local account.
Yes. Falconlite supports receiving and sending payments in major cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and USDC. You can also convert crypto to fiat currency instantly at competitive rates directly within the app.
Transfer limits vary by plan and verification level. Starter accounts can send up to $5,000/month. Growth accounts up to $50,000/month. Pro and Business accounts have higher or custom limits. You can increase your limits by completing enhanced verification in the app.

Security

Yes. Your funds are held in segregated accounts with our licensed banking partners and are never used for lending or investment. We use 256-bit AES encryption for all data, and our infrastructure is monitored 24/7 by our security team.
Yes. We strongly recommend enabling 2FA on your account. Falconlite supports authenticator apps (Google Authenticator, Authy) and SMS-based 2FA. You can enable 2FA in your account security settings.
If you notice any suspicious activity, freeze your card immediately from the app and contact our support team at support@falconlite.com. We have a dedicated fraud team that investigates all reports within 24 hours. You can also dispute transactions directly in the app.
Falconlite operates in compliance with applicable financial regulations in each jurisdiction we serve. Our banking partners are fully licensed and regulated. We adhere to strict AML (Anti-Money Laundering) and KYC (Know Your Customer) policies to protect our users and the financial system.
We take data privacy seriously. All personal data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell your data to third parties. You can review our full Privacy Policy for details on how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Account

Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen, enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link expires after 30 minutes for security. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
Each individual can have one personal Falconlite account. However, you can also create a separate Business account for your company. Business accounts have higher limits, team access controls, and additional features designed for businesses.
You can upgrade your plan at any time from the "Settings → Plan" section in the app. Upgrades take effect immediately and you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. Visit our Pricing page to compare plans.
To close your account, go to "Settings → Account → Close Account" in the app. Please ensure your balance is zero before closing. Any remaining funds must be withdrawn first. Account closure is permanent and cannot be undone. If you need help, contact our support team.
For personal accounts, you'll need a valid government-issued photo ID (national ID, passport, or driver's license) and a selfie for facial verification. For business accounts, you'll also need business registration documents and proof of address. The verification process is fully digital and typically takes under 10 minutes.
