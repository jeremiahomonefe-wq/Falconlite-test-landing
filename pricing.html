FAQ

Pricing Questions Answered

Everything you need to know before choosing a plan.

Yes, you can switch between Personal and Business pricing at any time from your account settings. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. Downgrading takes effect at the start of your next billing period.
Absolutely. Both the Growth and Pro plans come with a 14-day free trial — no credit card required. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period. If you decide not to continue, your account automatically reverts to the Starter (free) plan.
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), bank transfers, and cryptocurrency payments (USDT, USDC). You can also pay using your Falconlite wallet balance, which is the fastest and most convenient option for existing users.
No hidden fees — ever. The monthly subscription covers your plan features. Currency conversion uses transparent, published exchange rate spreads (0.5% on Growth, 0.25% on Pro). International wire transfers may incur a small flat fee of $2–$5 depending on the destination, which is always shown before you confirm.
If you reach your monthly transfer limit, you'll receive a notification and can either upgrade your plan instantly or wait for the limit to reset at the start of your next billing cycle. We never block transfers without warning — you'll always have the option to upgrade before a transaction is declined.
