User Stories

How Falconlite helped real people solve real financial challenges.

TN

Tunde Nwosu

Marketing Consultant · Lagos, Nigeria

The Challenge

Tunde was running a successful marketing consultancy with clients in the US and UK, but getting paid was a nightmare. Wire transfers took 5–7 days, banks charged exorbitant fees, and the exchange rates were terrible. He was losing up to 8% of every payment to fees and poor rates.

The Solution

Tunde signed up for Falconlite and set up his USD and GBP global accounts. He updated his invoices with his new account details and started receiving payments directly. The onboarding took less than 10 minutes and his first payment arrived the same day.

The Outcome

Tunde now saves over ₦200,000 per month in fees and exchange rate losses. Payments arrive within hours instead of days. He's expanded his client base to include companies in Canada and Australia, confident that getting paid will never be a problem again.

ZM

Zainab Musa

E-commerce Entrepreneur · Abuja, Nigeria

The Challenge

Zainab ran a thriving online store selling African fashion globally, but paying her international suppliers was a constant struggle. Her Nigerian bank card was frequently declined on international platforms, and the few payment options available charged fees that ate into her margins.

The Solution

Zainab got a Falconlite USD virtual card and linked it to her supplier accounts on Alibaba and other platforms. She also set up her multi-currency wallet to hold USD from her international sales, using it directly to pay suppliers without converting back and forth.

The Outcome

Zero payment declines since switching to Falconlite. Zainab has grown her supplier network from 3 to 12 international vendors, reduced her payment processing costs by 60%, and reinvested those savings into growing her inventory. Her business revenue has doubled in 12 months.
